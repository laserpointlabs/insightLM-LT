# Aircraft Development Project - Requirements Document

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Advanced Lightweight Transport (ALT-X1)
**Project Code:** ALT-X1-2025-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** January 2025

## Mission Requirements

### Primary Mission

Develop a next-generation lightweight transport aircraft capable of:

- Carrying 50-80 passengers
- Operating on short runways (minimum 1,200 meters)
- Fuel efficiency improvement of 30% over current generation
- Range: 2,500 nautical miles

### Performance Requirements

- **Cruise Speed:** Mach 0.75-0.80
- **Service Ceiling:** 41,000 feet
- **Takeoff Distance:** < 1,200 meters at sea level, ISA+15Â°C
- **Landing Distance:** < 1,000 meters
- **Maximum Payload:** 15,000 kg

## Safety Requirements

1. **Certification:** FAA Part 25 and EASA CS-25 compliance required
2. **Redundancy:** All critical systems must have triple redundancy
3. **Emergency Systems:** Must include emergency oxygen for all passengers and crew
4. **Fire Suppression:** Automatic fire detection and suppression in all compartments

## Environmental Requirements

- **Noise:** Meet ICAO Chapter 14 noise standards
- **Emissions:** Reduce CO2 emissions by 30% compared to baseline
- **Fuel:** Compatible with Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) up to 100%

## Technical Specifications

### Airframe

- **Material:** Advanced composite materials (carbon fiber reinforced polymer)
- **Wingspan:** 35 meters
- **Length:** 38 meters
- **Height:** 11.5 meters
- **Maximum Takeoff Weight:** 75,000 kg

### Propulsion

- **Engine Type:** High-bypass turbofan engines
- **Number of Engines:** 2
- **Thrust per Engine:** 35,000 lbf (155 kN)
- **Engine Manufacturer:** To be selected (RFQ issued)

### Avionics

- **Flight Management System:** Integrated FMS with GPS/INS navigation
- **Autopilot:** Category IIIB autoland capability
- **Displays:** Glass cockpit with 5 large-format displays
